Who We Are

Chaperone's Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation that primarily seeks to support less privileged school children in public primary and secondary schools across the Republic of Kenya. We do this by providing sanitary pads, decent school bags and mentorship programs. 

The organisation aims to fight inequality in education, low self-esteem among students in addition to creating a rich atmosphere for students to interact with and learn from professionals. We are currently providing sanitary pads to over 500 learners through a program that runs on a termly basis.

What We Do

We design and implement impactful initiatives that empower vulnerable groups, promote education, raise awareness on health and environmental issues, and provide financial support and mentorship, all with the goal of improving the lives and well-being of disadvantaged communities, including children, women, the elderly, and incarcerated individuals.

Empowering Vulnerable Groups​

We empower vulnerable groups through initiatives providing sanitary pads, mentorship for teen girls, elderly care, and enhancing overall well-being.

Supporting Education and Awareness

We promote education among children by providing school bags as well as raising awareness on lifestyle diseases and environmental care

Community and Economic Empowerment​

We provide community and economic empowerment through skill development, financial literacy programs, job creation, and fostering sustainable local businesses.

Health and Wellbeing

We promote health and wellbeing through programs like the provision of sanitary pads, elderly care and promoting awareness of lifestyle diseases
